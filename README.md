# Tip-calculator-Using-Js-Html
As a user, you went to a restaurant, based on your experience you wants to pay tip to the waiter.   For example if the experience is excellent and the total bill is 2000   The total tip would be 20% of 2000 i.e 400 Rs, if you are total 5 people, the per head tip would be 80Rs   You need to show 80 as an output.
